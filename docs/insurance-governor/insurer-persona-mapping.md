## The moat: Warranty Enforcement Engine

**Cross-platform mesh invariants** = automated policy warranty enforcement.

Insurers deny claims when insureds breach warranties (“maintain active MFA”, “no auto-payout above $X without SIU clearance”, “approved model version only”). Insurance Governor makes breach **runtime-impossible** when platforms share the spine.

See [warranty-enforcement-engine.md](warranty-enforcement-engine.md).
